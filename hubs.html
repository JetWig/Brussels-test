Brussels Airport

Brussels Airport, also known as Brussels Airport Zaventem, is the main international airport of Belgium.

19M+
Passengers in 2022
Location and Connectivity

Brussels Airport is located just 12 kilometers northeast of Brussels city center.

Modern Facilities

Brussels Airport features modern facilities and amenities, including a wide range of shops and restaurants, as well as a variety of services such as currency exchange and baggage storage.

Sustainability

Brussels Airport is committed to sustainability and has implemented several initiatives to reduce its environmental impact, including the use of solar panels, energy-efficient lighting, and waste reduction programs.

Safety and Security

Brussels Airport places a high priority on safety and security, with strict measures in place to ensure the safety of passengers and staff.
