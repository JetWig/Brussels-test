About Brussels Virtual

Brussels Virtual is a virtual airline that emulates the operations of Brussels Airlines, a leading Belgian airline with a rich history and strong reputation for quality and safety. As a virtual airline, we strive to replicate the real-world operations of Brussels Airlines in a simulated environment, providing our pilots with an authentic flying experience.

Brussels Airlines operates a fleet of modern aircraft to destinations across Europe, Africa, and North America, with a focus on offering exceptional service and comfort to passengers. At Brussels Virtual, we aim to provide our pilots with a similar level of service and comfort, with a range of virtual flights that reflect the real-world operations of Brussels Airlines.

We are proud to represent Brussels Airlines as a virtual airline and to offer our pilots the opportunity to experience the excitement and challenge of flying for a leading European airline. Our commitment to realism and attention to detail make Brussels Virtual an ideal choice for flight simmers who want to experience the thrill of flying for a real-world airline.
